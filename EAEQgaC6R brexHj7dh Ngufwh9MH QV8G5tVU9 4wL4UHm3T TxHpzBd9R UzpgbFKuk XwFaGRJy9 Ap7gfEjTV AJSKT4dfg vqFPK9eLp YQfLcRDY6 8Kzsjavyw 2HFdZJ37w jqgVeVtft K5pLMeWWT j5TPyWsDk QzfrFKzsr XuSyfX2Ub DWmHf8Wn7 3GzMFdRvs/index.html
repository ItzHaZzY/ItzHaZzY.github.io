Subject: UK terror suspects targeted U.S. Embassy London (SBU)

(SBU) Embassy London reports UK authorities announced today indictments for nine terror suspects arrested December

20. The indictments listed U.S. Embassy London as one of six targets the terrorists had planned to attack. Embassy

London notes there is not, and never was, an immediate threat to the embassy. The other five targets were St. Paul's

Cathedral, the London Stock Exchange, two Jewish community sites, and the Mayor of London.
